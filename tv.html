Panasonic TX-40CX680B TV

Lovely Ultra HD Firefox TV

We chose our new TV because it had FirefoxOS, how did that work out?
The screen

Most importantly the screen is exceptional. It's incredibly crisp, fast to update and very beautiful to look at. The factory setting is actually too good - watching TV shows that aren't UHD ready, or even recent films in HD you really see where effects or scenery come in, making suspension of disbelief hard. Luckily you can easily tweak the settings back to something "softer".

FirefoxOS

The apps

When we got the TV home the app selection was pretty poor. Before getting a smart TV we had our decent screen hooked up to a computer, so were used to having Netflix, Amazon Prime as well as iPlayer & other UK catch up services available. We got the TV a day after it launched so the apps available were pretty poor, and the only streaming service available was Netflix. Panasonic didn't have a timeline for the apps being made available but they did respond on twitter.
